Hello!


My name is Asarela. I'm an indonesian photographer currently based in Berlin. I started to take picture since i was 16 years old. Through pictures I can communicate better with people, tell stories and show emotions that I normaly can't do with words. The fact that i've lived in my hometown Indonesia for 18 years and then moved to Berlin, my way of thinking and seeing things have completely changed. My main interest are people and their culture. Every person is unique and i live to capture their story and uniqueness.

Experience


I'm currently doing a bachelor degree for Communication Design. My main focuses are film and photography.
When I was still living in Indonesia I did an internship by a well-known indonesian fashion photographer Dani Huda for 6 Months. Then I worked as a freelancer photographer for wedding, local fashion label's campaign, and local magazines.
In Germany i've done some collaborations with other students, designers and artists. I take pictures with both digital (Canon 5D Mark II) and analog (Contax G2).

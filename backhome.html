A Trip Backhome


I was 18 years old when decided to move abroad. Le the idea of comfort zone with hope it’s only for awhile. I still got familiar places and faces captured in memories. And last year, I thought, I was lucky enough to get a chance to relive all those memories back. I was home. Yet home is di erent. Home is changed and I was confused. I lost in places I used to make stories in. Didn’t understand words from faces I used to share stories with. I was looking and looking for some pieces I might t in but I was totally lost. When you lost you tend to be more sensitive to what’s around you. Look at the scenes that you won’t look at before. Start to observe things that wouldn’t even bother you back then. It felt so strange yet exciting, It opened my eyes to some new layers of feeling and understanding of what i used to call “home”.

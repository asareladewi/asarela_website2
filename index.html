Elevator

A short film project as an assignment from HTW Berlin. Created by a team of 7 students. The assignment comes with a line "Try to create a movie that doesn't make sense.". This film tried to hold the idea of absurdity inside a 2 square meters elevator. The marble is the key to all nonsense that happened inside this cubicle. At the end, even the most absurd event does make some kind of sense, more or less.

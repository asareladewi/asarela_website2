Video Project


When a picture tells a thousand things, moving pictures will take you to another world of imaginations fullfilled with thousand emotions

Elevator

A short film project as an assignment from HTW Berlin. Created by a team of 7 students. The assignment comes with a line "Try to create a movie that doesn't make sense.". This film tried to hold the idea of absurdity inside a 2 square meters elevator. The marble is the key to all nonsense that happened inside this cubicle. At the end, even the most absurd event does make some kind of sense, more or less.

A main project as an assignment from Prof. Andreas Ingerl (HTW-Berlin) created by me and my two friends, Sarah Bracher and Tetyana Gryniva. The assignment was to visualize one of Daniel Charm's literature works into a short movie. We chose his text called "Traum", took the main idea and emotion out of it, which are angst and paranoia. Then create our own version of "Traum". Besides the video takes, we did also put a bit of 3-D motion elements into it.
